Convert anonymous website visitors into valuable leads!

== Description ==

Websitez looks at every visitor that comes to your website and turns them into a valuable lead.

Use this information to customize your website for the visitor such as offering coupons or target them on performance marketing websites like Google!

Get the leads right inside your WordPress administration area.

*BENEFITS*

* Over 90% of website traffic stays anonymous. Find out who they are.
* One click installation!
* View the leads right inside your dashboard.
* Completely free!
* Customize your website based on who the visitor is!
* Use this information to do performance marketing on sites like LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Google, Instagram, etc.

Stop losing out on all the leads that are already visiting your website!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How does it work? =

A JavaScript snippet is inserted on every page of your website. When a visitor visits your website, they are identified using the Websitez.com service.

The only information used is the IP address of the user. More information can be found here: https://websitez.com/privacy-policy

= How long does it take to see the leads? =

The leads are near real-time, and will show up typically in less than a minute. In some instances it can take up to 30 minutes.
